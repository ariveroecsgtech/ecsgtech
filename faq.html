FAQ

🧩 Services Management (Managed Services) – FAQs
General (Managed Services)1) What’s included in your Managed Services?

24/7 monitoring, patching, endpoint security oversight, backup monitoring, user support, asset management/reporting, and guidance for technology decisions. We tailor the scope to your goals and budget.2) Do you support hybrid environments (on‑prem + cloud)?

Yes. We design and operate hybrid environments (on‑prem servers, Azure, Microsoft 365) with consistent policies and security.3) How do you price Managed Services?

Predictable monthly pricing per user/device/scope. Includes SLAs, reporting, and scheduled reviews.4) How fast is onboarding?

Typically 1–2 weeks after discovery. Includes documentation, secure access, RMM agents, and patch/security standards.5) Can you work with our internal IT team?

Absolutely. We offer co‑managed IT with clear swim lanes and shared dashboards.6) What SLAs do you provide?

Response and resolution targets by priority (impact/urgency), plus monthly service reports.

Cybersecurity1) How do you protect against phishing and ransomware?

Layered security: email security, DNS filtering, MFA, EDR/NGAV, user awareness training, and 24/7 monitoring (SOC).2) Do you offer a Security Operations Center (SOC)?

Yes—continuous monitoring, event correlation, and escalation of verified incidents.3) Can you help with compliance (HIPAA, GLBA, etc.)?

We align policies, controls, and retention with your requirements and provide audit‑friendly reporting.4) How often do you run security reviews?

Monthly or quarterly depending on risk and contract.5) Do you provide simulated phishing?

Yes—regular campaigns with reporting and targeted follow‑up training.6) What’s your incident response approach?

Immediate containment, root‑cause analysis, secure recovery, and remediation to prevent recurrence.

vCIO1) What does a vCIO do?

Defines strategy and roadmap, builds budgets, sets policies, manages vendors, and prioritizes IT investments aligned to business goals.2) How often do we meet?

Typically monthly/quarterly (QBRs) with metrics, risks, and next steps.3) Can you help with budgeting?

Yes—12–36 month forecasts, lifecycle planning, and license optimization.4) Do you handle vendor evaluations?

Yes—comparable RFPs, demos, fit‑gap, and negotiation focused on value and risk.

Cybersecurity Training1) What formats do you offer?

Onsite/virtual sessions, 1:1 coaching, small groups, town halls, and ongoing programs.2) How do you measure effectiveness?

Quizzes, completion reports, phishing test results, and risk trend analysis.3) Can training be tailored by department or role?

Yes—role‑based content for Finance, Sales, HR, field teams, admins, etc.

Backup & Disaster Recovery1) What’s the difference between backup and disaster recovery (DR)?

Backup protects your data copies; DR restores systems and operations to meet RPO/RTO objectives.2) How often do you test restores?

Automated restore tests with periodic validations; we share the results.3) Do you cover Microsoft 365 data?

Yes—Exchange, OneDrive, SharePoint, and Teams.

Remote Monitoring & Management (RMM)1) What does RMM monitor?

Servers, PCs, network devices, services, patch status, AV/EDR health, performance, and critical alerts.2) Will maintenance disrupt users?

We schedule maintenance windows and use automation to minimize impact.3) Do you provide reports?

Yes—system health, patching, incidents, and recommendations.

☁️ Cloud Services – FAQs
Azure1) What workloads can you move to Azure?

VMs, databases, line‑of‑business apps, storage, backup, and DR.2) How do you control Azure costs?

Rightsizing, policies, budgets/alerts, reserved instances, and tagging.3) Is hybrid identity supported?

Yes—Microsoft Entra ID (formerly Azure AD), synchronization, MFA/Conditional Access.4) How do you secure Azure?

Zero‑trust principles, RBAC, policy‑as‑code, private networking, encryption, and posture management.

SharePoint Data Storage1) How do you structure sites and libraries?

By departments/projects with metadata, retention, and least‑privilege permissions.2) Can you migrate from file shares or Google Drive?

Yes—with permission mapping, version retention, and post‑migration cleanup.3) Is external sharing safe?

Yes—with guest controls, sensitivity labels, DLP, and policy enforcement.

Email Security1) Do you implement DMARC/DKIM/SPF?

Yes—along with advanced filtering, attachment/URL sandboxing, and DLP.2) Can you stop impersonation (BEC) attacks?

Yes—impersonation controls, executive protection, and user awareness prompts.3) Do you provide email encryption?

Yes—policy‑based, automated encryption and labeling.

Microsoft 365 Email (Exchange Online)1) What migration paths do you support?

Cutover, staged, or hybrid from Exchange/IMAP/Google.2) Will users lose email during migration?

We use coexistence or well‑planned cutovers in low‑impact windows to maintain continuity.3) Do you offer backups for Microsoft 365 email?

Yes—with granular restore and retention policies.

Microsoft Intune1) Do you support BYOD and COPE?

Yes—with MDM/MAM, data separation, and selective wipe.2) What compliance checks can you enforce?

Encryption, OS version, active antivirus, and non‑rooted/non‑jailbroken status.3) Can you automate device onboarding?

Yes—Windows Autopilot, profiles, app deployment, and update rings.

Microsoft Teams1) Can you deploy Teams Phone?

Yes—PSTN connectivity, auto attendants, call queues, and policies.2) How do you control sprawl in Teams?

Naming conventions, lifecycle policies, templates, and governance.3) Do you train users on Teams?

Yes—role‑based training with quick‑reference guides and recordings (when permitted).

🛠️ Project Management – FAQs
Camera Installations1) Do you provide site surveys and coverage maps?

VPN, role‑based permissions, and hardened configurations.3) What about storage and retention policies?

Structured cabling, switching, firewalls/VPN, Wi‑Fi design, VLANs, QoS, and documentation.2) Do you provide Wi‑Fi heatmaps and validation?

Yes—design for density/roaming and on‑site validation tests.3) How do you plan network cutovers?

After‑hours windows, rollback plans, and proactive communications.

Training (Individual & Group)1) Which topics do you cover?

Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Teams, SharePoint), cybersecurity awareness, Intune/admin basics, and department‑specific workflows.2) Can sessions be recorded?

Yes, when permitted; we also provide cheat‑sheets and follow‑ups.3) How do you measure success?

Surveys, adoption metrics, and knowledge checks.

Yes—hypercare, fine‑tuning, and cleanup of broken links/duplicates.
