⭐ What Is BullPhish ID?

BullPhish ID is a powerful platform designed for MSPs that combines security awareness training with automated phishing simulations to educate employees and measure their real‑world readiness. It offers a wide catalog of training modules, realistic phishing templates, and detailed reporting to help organizations stay ahead of evolving cyber threats.
The platform is constantly updated with new courses and phishing kits to reflect the latest attack methods and compliance requirements.
