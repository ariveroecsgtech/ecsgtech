Scope / What’s Included

Site survey & coverage map (entrances, high‑traffic areas, critical assets)
Camera selection (fixed, varifocal, PTZ, indoor/outdoor, IR, PoE)
Recording/storage design (NVR, retention policy, storage sizing)
Mounting, structured cabling, labeling, and patch‑panel work
VLANs, QoS & bandwidth planning for video traffic
Camera/NVR configuration, motion zones, alerts & user roles
Remote/mobile secure access & multi‑site views
Documentation (as‑builts, IP schema, device inventory)
Knowledge transfer & user training

Project Approach

Assessment: site walk‑through, risk & coverage analysis
Design: camera layout, network plan, storage & retention
Build: cabling, mounting, PoE/switching, configuration
Validate: image quality, angles, retention, alerting
Handover: documentation, training, support plan

Deliverables

Camera layout drawings & device list
NVR/storage configuration & retention policy
Access control list (roles & permissions)
Test/acceptance report & user guides
